Privacy Notice

All analysis runs locally in your browser. No images or video frames are sent to external servers.

If you connect to the optional Python backend, frames are processed on your local machine only.

Results are stored in your browser's IndexedDB. Use "Clear All Data" to remove everything.

About

Expression Analyser

Version 2.0.0

AI-powered facial expression analysis platform for meetings, video uploads, and real-time webcam detection. Detects 7 emotions with high precision using face-api.js (client-side) and optional DeepFace/YOLOv8 backend.

Technology Stack

face-api.js — Client-side face detection
Flask + DeepFace — Backend (optional)
YOLOv8 — Advanced detection
Chart.js — Data visualization
IndexedDB — Client-side storage
WebRTC — Multi-participant meetings
